Construction Councillor

The Construction Councillor:
  • Allows for the automatic upgrade of all city buildings, or specific city buildings, once you achieve the title of Commander
  • Increases your Build queue slots from 6 to 16
  • Allows construction orders to be placed in the Build queue, even if the city does not have all of the resources required to begin construction:
  • The Councillor order will sit in the queue until it reaches the top of the queue
  • At this point either the resources will be spent to begin construction of the Councillor order, or if there are not enough resources to begin the order, the next order in the queue will begin construction
  • You can also select to keep the queue order fixed, so that the next order does not begin construction, if there are not enough resources available to begin the previous order
  • Once you have enough resources available, you can also manually convert a Councillor order into a normal order

Defense Councillor

The Defense Councillor:
  • Allows for the automatic enlistment of units, the numbers of which you specify, up to your troop capacity
  • If you come under attack, provides information to your alliance members about which of your cities is under attack, at what time, and by whom
  • Allows for the automatic upgrade of defense buildings (posts, barricades, and walls) once you achieve the title of Commander
  • Increases your Enlistment queue from 6 to 16 slots
  • Troops Overview

War Councillor

The War Councillor:
  • Allows you to raid a cavern repeatedly, using a pre-selected troop amount
  • Coming Soon: Allows you to set a repeating raid to return home at a certain time
  • Allows you to set a time for your troops to execute a command, either by departure time from their city, or arrival time at their destination
  • Easily enables you to find caverns near your city, to send troops to
  • Easily shows you which of your cities contain idle units

Trade Councillor

The Trade Councillor:
  • Can be set to send anything over a set amount of resources to another city in your empire
  • Can be set to request anything up to a set amount of resources from another city in your empire
  • Will automatically refine any resources that a city does not have storage for, if there is a level 10 Sorcerer's Tower available

Requesting a Substitute:
A Substitute can play your account for you for a period of time. The maximum amount of time any substitute can play your account for you, is 40% of total days played.
When selecting a player to give substitute rights to, be sure to pick someone trustworthy! Once a player accepts your substitute request, he/she will be able to chat, mail, and post in alliance forums as you. He/she will also be able to upgrade and downgrade your buildings, as well as send your troops to reinforce others. However, your substitute will not be able to send PvP attacks, or do any trading.

Reset Game:
If you feel that you've got no chance of success in your current gameplay situation, you can reset your game, so you are placed on another continent with 1 brand new city.
Upon starting a server, you cannot reset your game for 48 hours. Once you have reset your game, you cannot reset again for 48 hours.
Resetting your game will abandon all of your current cities, and you will lose all of your titles, gold, refined resources, research levels, and all stored items. You will begin the game again with 1 city spawned in a random location. You will also have this reset listed in your Player History, which will follow you on all worlds that you join later.
